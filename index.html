Where it all started

The idea for the game of a little neutrino finding its way back to the sun was inspired by one of the teachers at Lighthouse Labs. After a lecture, Christian Nally was talking about his previous job experiences and he went on to explain his time working at The Sudbury Neutrino Observatory. After learning about neutrinos, Zoey (one of the developers) combined her love of video games and coding to come up with a video game demo: Lighthouse Labyrinth.

Who

Lighthouse Labyrinth was brought to you by Zoey Farago, Jaime Reuss, and Alex Van Der Voort, three passionate and creative web developers.

Credits

The demo for Lighthouse Labyrinth was developed with Phaser 3 a JavaScript game engine. Sound from Zapsplat and SoundBible. Fragment Shader source code for WebGL Pipelines from Phaser 2 examples.
